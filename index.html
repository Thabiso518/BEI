Maburu Laptop Services App

Built by Students, for Students

Business Overview

Business Concept

Maburu is a new technology support service designed specifically for university students. Think of it as a friendly, tech-savvy friend who is always available to help with any laptop problem.

Who We Are

Maburu is a team of young, passionate students and recent graduates based near the University of Lesotho. Because we are also students, we deeply understand the struggles and frustrations that come with laptop issues.

What We Do

  • Software Installation (Microsoft Office, Zoom, antivirus)
  • Laptop Troubleshooting and Repair
  • Affordable Laptop Sales with Payment Plans
  • Remote Support via App

Market Segmentation

We target students through:

  • Demographic: Age 18-24, students and academic staff
  • Geographic: National University of Lesotho and surrounding colleges
  • Psychographic: Tech-dependent, budget-conscious students
  • Behavioral: Those needing flexible payment plans and quick solutions

Market Size Estimation

Total Addressable Market (TAM): LSL 2.55 million

Serviceable Available Market (SAM): LSL 120,000 (Year 1 estimate)

Value Proposition & Solutions

Why Focus on First-Year Students?

First-year students are in a critical transition period. Their laptop is their most important tool for learning. By helping them immediately, we build a relationship of trust that lasts throughout their university life.

Our Promise to Students

  • Speed: On-campus location means faster service
  • Affordability: Student-friendly pricing
  • Convenience: Request help 24/7 from anywhere
  • Flexibility: Payment plans over 3 months

Conclusion

Maburu is more than just a business—it is a vital academic support system for students.
